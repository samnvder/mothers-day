A relaxed afternoon of food prepared with care — and time to be together.

To every mother and mother figure in our community: thank you. For the patience, the late nights, the small moments that add up to a life — we see you, and we are grateful.

On Mother’s Day, we invite you to step into The Lounge for a buffet-style luncheon: seasonal flowers on the tables, dishes meant to feel generous rather than hurried, and a setting where you can simply enjoy the people at your side.

Members are welcome to bring guests, including friends who are not yet members. When you are ready, a completed RSVP and prepayment help us hold your table — we assign seating in the order reservations are received.

Buffet style, all you can eat.

A few favorites guests often return to — everything below is on our Mother’s Day menu.

• Carving station: oven-roasted rib eye
• Seafood: grilled salmon & shrimp scampi
• From the grill: pork BBQ ribs & grilled chicken breast
• Salad bar & sides: salad station, mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, rolls, and more
• Something sweet: cheesecake bites, brownie bites, cookies & fresh fruit
• For little ones: chicken tenders, cheese & pepperoni pizza

At a glance

• Included beverages (included all day): fresh squeezed orange juice, soda, and lemonade.
• Bar & optional add-ons: one comped house wine (Angeline Cabernet or Chardonnay) or one bottled beer; bottomless mimosas (+$25 per adult) and a full bar.
• Specialty cocktails: Mom’s Rosé Spritz ($11), Garden Party Margarita ($12), House Margarita $8.
• Pricing: $120 per adult · $40 for children ages 5–12 (gratuity included). Children under 5 may dine at no charge when noted on the RSVP form.
